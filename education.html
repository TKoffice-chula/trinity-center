Education & Training

Building global capacity in kidney care through accredited fellowships, certifications, and continuing medical education.

Pathways to Excellence

Fellowship

Clinical Nephrology Fellowship

A two-year mentored fellowship combining hands-on clinical training with research and quality improvement projects.

Certificate

Dialysis Care Certification

A structured certification for nurses and technicians providing hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis services.

CME

Online Nephrology Academy

Self-paced, accredited modules covering the spectrum of clinical nephrology — free for clinicians worldwide.

Workshop

Renal Pathology Master Course

An intensive workshop on kidney biopsy interpretation, led by international experts.

Symposium

Annual Trinity Symposium

Our flagship event bringing together leading clinicians and scientists from around the world.

Mentorship

Early Investigator Mentorship

One-on-one mentorship pairing early-career researchers with established nephrology investigators.

Why Train With Us

Our programs combine rigorous academic content with real-world clinical exposure. Trainees join a global community of practice that extends far beyond their formal study period.

Every Trinity Center program is built around evidence-based curricula, regularly updated to reflect the latest advances in kidney disease management and research.
