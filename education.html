Education & Capacity Building

Beyond procedural training — Trinity Center-ISN runs structured courses, fellowships, textbooks, and digital learning resources for the global nephrology community.

Structured Programs

Fellowship

Trinity Center-ISN Interventional Nephrology Fellowship

Two-week intensive procedural training covering HD catheter, PD catheter, kidney biopsy, and POCUS. See the Training page for full details.

PD Joint Academy

ISPD & ISPD-APC Fellowship

The PD Joint Training Academy in collaboration with the International Society for Peritoneal Dialysis and its Asia-Pacific Chapter — annual cohorts since 2024.

Nurse Training

PD Nurse Training Course

National-scale PD Nurse Training: 6 weeks theory, 10 weeks skill development, plus 1-week PD Camp. Endorsed by Thai Red Cross Society, NST, MOPH, TNC, and TNNA.

Procedural Course

CU-Tenckhoff Catheter Insertion Course

Focused hands-on course on Tenckhoff (PD) catheter insertion technique, exit-site construction, and post-insertion care.

Forum

Asia Nephrology & Transplant Forum

Regional academic forum bringing together nephrologists from Cambodia, Hong Kong, India, Indonesia, Lao PDR, Malaysia, Philippines, Singapore, Taiwan, Thailand, and Vietnam.

Policy Learning

Policy Makers & International Fellows Program

Hosting health-system policy leaders from Ethiopia, Indonesia, Laos, Myanmar, Nepal, Philippines, and Vietnam to share Thailand's national PD program experience.

Learning Resources

Open-access educational content created and curated by our team.

Textbook

PD Textbook

Two editions of a Thai-language peritoneal dialysis textbook covering the full clinical spectrum — from patient selection through long-term complication management.

Handout

PD Handout

Patient-facing handouts and clinician quick-reference guides used across the Trinity hospital network and beyond.

Video Series

"Cooking with..." — 5 Chapters

YouTube series on kidney care fundamentals with subtitles in 4 languages:

  • Ch. 1 – Kidney disease basics
  • Ch. 2 – CKD & treatment options
  • Ch. 3 – Peritoneal dialysis
  • Ch. 4 – PD-related problems & initial management
  • Ch. 5 – Diet & nutrition for CKD/PD patients
Case Series

OSEA Case Discussion (YouTube)

17 educational videos covering real-world nephrology case discussions for clinicians and trainees — available open access.
