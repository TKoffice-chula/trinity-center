About Trinity Center

A center of excellence dedicated to advancing kidney care through research, education, and global academic collaboration.

Our Mission

Sustainably Elevating Kidney Care for All

Trinity Center exists to transform the way kidney disease is understood, treated, and prevented. We combine clinical excellence with rigorous science and a global network of partners to develop scalable solutions to one of medicine's most pressing challenges.

Our work spans three pillars: developing the next generation of nephrology knowledge through research; building clinical and academic capacity through training and education; and convening international collaborations that translate evidence into impact.

Our Vision

A world where every person living with kidney disease has equitable access to high-quality, evidence-based care — regardless of geography or income.

Our Values

Excellence · Equity · Integrity · Collaboration · Innovation

Our Story

A Journey of Impact

2018

Founding of Trinity Center

Established by a coalition of nephrologists, researchers, and academic institutions to address the global kidney care gap.

2020

Launch of the Trinity Fellowship

Our flagship clinical fellowship program welcomed its first cohort of nephrology trainees from 12 countries.

2022

Online Nephrology Academy

Free, accredited CME courses made available to more than 8,000 clinicians worldwide.

2024

Global Kidney Health Atlas

Published a landmark report mapping kidney care infrastructure across 130+ countries.

2026

Pediatric Nephrology Initiative

Launched a dedicated program to address the underserved pediatric kidney care population.
