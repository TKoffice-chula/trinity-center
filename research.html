Research & Guidelines

Translating science into impact — our research advances the understanding, prevention, and treatment of kidney disease.

Where We Focus

Clinical

Risk stratification, progression modeling, and novel therapeutic strategies for CKD management.

Translational

Biomarker discovery and precision medicine approaches to AKI prevention and recovery.

Health Systems

Quality improvement, access equity, and outcome optimization for kidney replacement therapy.

Epidemiology

Global Kidney Health

Mapping disease burden, care gaps, and health policy across low- and middle-income countries.

Pediatric

Pediatric Nephrology

Congenital kidney disease, growth, and lifelong outcomes for children with renal conditions.

AI & Data

Digital Health Innovation

Machine learning models for risk prediction, registry analytics, and telehealth applications.

Global Kidney Health Atlas

A landmark multi-year project mapping kidney care availability, infrastructure, and outcomes across more than 130 countries — providing actionable data to inform health policy worldwide.

The Atlas brings together data from national registries, surveys of clinicians, and patient-reported outcomes to identify gaps and opportunities for improving access to care.
